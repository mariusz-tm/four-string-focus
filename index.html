Who and What is Four-String Focus?

Hello and welcome to Four-String Focus!
Four-String Focus is a (university project) website about everything bass related (except the fish)!

Based on Statista data estimated with NAMM ratios, despite there being an increase of revenue from instruments (more instruments being sold yearly), electric guitars outsell bass guitars by ~3-4x consistantly!

To counter-act this disparity, this website teaches the wonders of the bass guitar through the following topics;

  1. A brief history about bass guitars, from its initial creation and use, to how its utilized in the modern age.
  2. Some notable bassists throughout history, their unique playing styles and famous basslines.
  3. And lastly, famous bass guitar brands and where to find them!

Please enjoy your time browsing our website! You can use both the top and bottom navigation to visit each section mentioned above. On the right-hand side of the page, you can see the "Bassist of the Day", who currently is CURRENT BASSIST, with some information and one of their famous basslines!

This site is constantly changing and updating! If you don't see your favourite bassist, most-known brands, or fun facts about bass guitar history, contact us!
