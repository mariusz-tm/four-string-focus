Who and What is Four-String Focus?

Hello and welcome to Four-String Focus!
Four-String Focus is a (project) website about everything bass related (except the fish)!
More specifically, the bass-related topics that we currently cover are;

• A brief history about bass guitars, from its initial creation and use, to how its utilized in the modern age.
• Some notable bassists throughout history, their unique playing styles and famous basslines.
• And lastly, famous bass guitar brands and where to find them!

Please enjoy your time browsing our website! You can use both the top and bottom navigation to visit each section mentioned above. On the right-hand side of the page, you can see the "Bassist of the Day", who currently is CURRENT BASSIST, with some information and one of their famous basslines!

This site is constantly changing and updating! If you don't see your favourite bassist, most-known brands, or fun facts about bass guitar history, contact us!
